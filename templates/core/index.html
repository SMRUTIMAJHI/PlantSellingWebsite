Services

At Lilis, we offer a full range of services to make your plant journey smooth, enjoyable, and successful. Here’s how we help you cultivate your green space:

Plant Recommendations & Personalized Care Plans 🌱

Unsure where to start? We provide personalized plant recommendations based on your space, lifestyle, and preferences. Our care plans are tailored to help you grow happy, healthy plants.

Plant Health Consultations 🩺

Have a struggling plant? Our plant health experts are here to diagnose issues and offer guidance on everything from pest control to repotting and revival techniques.

Custom Planting & Design Services 🏡

Looking to create an indoor jungle or transform your outdoor area? Our design experts can help you plan, choose, and place the right plants for any space, bringing beauty and wellness to your environment.

Workshops & Educational Events 🎓

Join our workshops to learn hands-on skills like propagation, plant care, and seasonal planting tips. We also host virtual and in-person events, bringing the plant community together to share, learn, and grow.

Plant Maintenance Services 🧑‍🌾

For businesses, homes, or special events, our team provides regular plant maintenance, including watering, pruning, and pest management, to keep your green spaces looking their best.

Gift Services 🎁

Give the gift of green! Our plant gifting service makes it easy to surprise friends, family, or coworkers with beautiful plants, complete with care instructions and personalized notes.
